Design Mockups

Mockups are used in various projects to share with various teams throughout Ascend Learning. The Instructional Designers, Writers, QA, and Animation/Design teams refer to these mockups when creating and reviewing the motion graphics throughout the sim. Here are a couple of small projects that are a part of a larger project I'm currently working on based on health issues.

Engage Community Health: Environmental Influences

This project helps users identify environmental health triggers by engaging in a simulated conversation between a nurse and a family discovering what could be causing their family's respiratory issues.

Engage Community Health: Violence and Abuse

This project focuses on abuse that the youth and elderly may encounter at school or where they live. Users will go through a simulated conversation that showcases the proper steps they need to take when they know someone experiencing abuse.
