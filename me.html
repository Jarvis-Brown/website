Hello!

I am an Animator and a Fullstack designer in Philadelphia, PA with roots in St.Louis, MO. I'm passionate about working on designs and animations that improve lives and have a positive social impact. I design in a human-centric way, incorporating user research and practical choices for user interfaces. I draw design inspiration from my favorite brands and trends, using my Master's in Fine Arts, to add a creative touch to everything I do.

Animation Work

Early on in life, I knew I wanted to be an artist and bring my drawings to life in a digital format. I started my career in animation after completing my studies at the Chicago Illinois Institute of the Arts. I traveled from Chicago to San Francisco in pursuit of animation and received my MFA from the Academy of Art. Afterward, I moved between LA and the Bay Area working on various projects before moving to New York where I've worked as a character animator and motion graphics designer for the last 4 years.

Design Work

After working in the Animation field for 8 years, I decided to pursue a career as a Fullstack Designer to grow my artistic skillset further. I attended the CareerFoundry to gain certification in UI Design and have been currently working on projects to diversify my portfolio. After completing certification I attended Coding Dojo to gain certification in Fullstack Development.

Hobbies

In my downtime, I enjoy gaming, swimming, painting, drawing, and researching the newest techniques in digital art. On any given weekend I can be found walking around my newest city, visiting museums, and finding inspiration in the world around me.
